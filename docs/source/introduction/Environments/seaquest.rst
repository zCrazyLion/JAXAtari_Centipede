Description
-------------
You control a submarine that can move in all directions and fire torpedoes. The objective is to rescue as many divers as possible while avoiding and destroying enemy submarines and sharks. The sub has a limited oxygen supply that depletes at a constant rate — surfacing periodically is required to replenish it. Failing to surface in time destroys the sub and costs a diver. Surfacing with fewer than six divers aboard also costs one diver. Collisions with any non-allied entity destroy the sub. The game starts with one active sub and three in reserve; an additional sub is awarded for every 10,000 points scored, up to a maximum of six reserves.
